Weekly Workout Routine

Day-wise split with posture tips and images.

Monday — Back

Pull-ups

Full hang to chin over bar; controlled descent. 3×max

Lat Pulldown

Sit tall, pull to upper chest; scapula retraction. 3×8–12

Seated Cable Row

Squeeze mid-back; no torso swing. 3×8–12

Deadlift

Hinge hips, neutral spine, core tight. 3–5×4–6

Tuesday — Chest

Bench Press

Scapula retracted; bar to mid-chest. 3–4×6–10

Incline Dumbbell Press

Bench 20–30°; controlled arc. 3×8–12

Chest Fly

Light elbow bend; controlled ROM. 3×10–15

Push-ups

Straight line; full depth. 3×max or 4×12

Wednesday — Legs

Squats

Weight in heels; knees track toes. 4×6–10

Leg Press

Controlled range; no lockout. 3×8–12

Lunges

Step length keeps knee over ankle. 3×10 each leg

Hamstring Curl

Full contraction; controlled eccentric. 3×10–15

Thursday — Shoulders

Overhead Press

Neutral spine; elbows slightly forward. 4×5–8

Lateral Raises

Slight bend in elbows; pause at top. 3×10–12

Front Raises

Controlled lift; don't swing. 3×10–12

Shrugs

Squeeze at top; hold 1 second. 3×10–15

Friday — Arms

Biceps Curls

Controlled movement; 2-sec eccentric. 3×8–12

Triceps Pushdown

Full extension; smooth cable path. 3×10–15

Hammer Curls

Neutral grip; peak contraction. 3×8–12

Skull Crushers

Behind head; elbows fixed. 3×8–12

Saturday — Cardio & Core

Plank Hold

Neutral spine; glutes tight; breathe. 3×30–60 sec

Leg Raises

Controlled; lower back on floor. 3×10–15

Russian Twists

Seated, lean back 45°, rotate. 3×20

Cardio Circuit

Running, cycling, or rowing at 70–80% HR. 20–30 min
